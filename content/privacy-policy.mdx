# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: October 4, 2025**

## Introduction

Welcome to Pomodoro Timer ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Pomodoro Timer mobile application (the "App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

Our App is designed with privacy in mind. We do **not** collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users.

### Automatically Collected Information

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Timer Settings**: Your customized work session, short break, and long break durations
- **Session Statistics**: Number of completed Pomodoro sessions
- **App Preferences**: Your app configuration and settings
- **Update Preferences**: Your choice regarding automatic app update checks

**Important**: All data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

## How We Use Your Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

- Maintain your timer preferences between app sessions
- Track your productivity statistics
- Provide a personalized user experience
- Remember your app settings

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms:
- iOS: AsyncStorage (encrypted at the OS level)
- Android: AsyncStorage (encrypted at the OS level)

### No Cloud Sync

We do **not**:
- Upload your data to any servers
- Sync your data across devices
- Share your data with third parties
- Use analytics or tracking services
- Display advertisements

## Third-Party Services

### App Updates

The App uses your device'(')pple App Store or Google Play Store) to check for and install updates. This functionality is provided by:

- **iOS**: Apple App Store
- **Android**: Google Play Store

Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy for information on how they handle update checks.

### No Third-Party Analytics

We do **not** use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

## Children's Privacy

Our App does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. The App is safe for users of all ages.

## Data Retention

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

- Data persists until you uninstall the app
- You can manually reset all data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app
- No data is retained by us after app uninstallation

## Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: All your data is accessible within the app
- **Delete**: Uninstall the app to delete all data
- **Modify**: Change settings directly in the app
- **Export**: Data is stored locally and not available for export (no cloud storage)

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your locally stored data:

- Data is stored using device-level encryption
- No network transmission of personal data
- No server-side storage
- No third-party access

## International Data Transfers

Since we do not collect or transmit any data, there are no international data transfers.

## California Privacy Rights

If you are a California resident, you have specific rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

- **Right to Know**: We do not collect personal information
- **Right to Delete**: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
- **Right to Opt-Out**: Not applicable as we don't sell personal information

## European Union (GDPR) Compliance

For users in the European Union:

- We do not process personal data as defined by GDPR
- All data storage is local to your device
- No data controllers or processors are involved
- No cookies are used

## Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

**Email**: support@pomodorotimer.app
**Website**: https://pomodorotimer.app
